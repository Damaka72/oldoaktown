Development Progress & Timeline

Track the transformation of 140 hectares into West London's newest neighbourhood - 25,500 homes, 65,000 jobs, and the UK's best-connected transport hub.

🚄 HS2 Station

Investment: £1.7 billion

Size: 14 platforms, 850m roof span

Capacity: 250,000 passengers daily

Status: Under Construction

Opening: 2033+

The UK's largest newly built station, connecting HS2, Elizabeth Line, Great Western Main Line, and Heathrow Express.

🏘️ Housing

Total Homes: 25,500 planned

Affordable: 50% of all homes

First Phase: 500 homes approved

Status: Planning & Construction

Timeline: 2027-2039

Creating one of London's most ambitious mixed-tenure communities with genuine affordable housing integration.

💼 Employment

New Jobs: 65,000 planned

Current Jobs: 40,000 (Park Royal)

Commercial Space: 2.5 million sq ft

Status: Masterplanning

Timeline: 2028-2039

Building on Park Royal's industrial legacy with modern workspace, tech hubs, and diverse employment opportunities.

🌳 Green Space

Parks: 40 hectares planned

Grand Union Canal: Enhanced towpath

Biodiversity: Protected habitats

Status: Design Phase

Timeline: 2026-2035

Creating one of London's greenest new neighbourhoods with extensive parks, waterways, and sustainable design.

Key Development Areas

Old Oak North

Focus: Residential & Community

High-density mixed-tenure housing with excellent access to the HS2 station. Includes schools, healthcare, and community facilities.

Homes: 8,000+ | Timeline: 2027-2035

Old Oak South

Focus: Mixed-Use Gateway

Commercial and residential mix creating a vibrant gateway district around the station with retail, dining, and entertainment.

Homes: 6,000+ | Timeline: 2028-2036

Park Royal

Focus: Employment & Innovation

Transformation of existing industrial estate into modern workspace, tech clusters, and innovation districts while preserving key industries.

Jobs: 40,000+ | Timeline: 2025-2039

Grand Union Canal

Focus: Leisure & Waterfront

Enhanced canal-side development with waterfront homes, towpath improvements, cafes, and leisure facilities creating a unique urban oasis.

Homes: 3,000+ | Timeline: 2029-2038

Scrubs Lane

Focus: Creative Industries

Building on the area's TV and media heritage with studio space, creative workspace, and homes for creative professionals.

Homes: 4,000+ | Timeline: 2030-2037

Wormwood Scrubs

Focus: Green Connection

Enhancing connections to this historic open space with improved access, ecological corridors, and community green initiatives.

Status: Protected Space | Timeline: Ongoing
